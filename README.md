## Scope and limitations

- This repository does not host original media files.
- Descriptions are limited to observable events visible in the referenced material.
- Information may be incomplete or subject to later correction.
- Inclusion of a link does not constitute legal attribution of responsibility.

## Use

Links from this repository may be referenced in academic work, media reporting, or correspondence with public officials and international bodies.
